Efficient Gradient Methods for Large-Scale Optimization

Author A, Your Name, Author C
International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2025

Abstract. We propose a new family of gradient methods that achieve near-optimal convergence rates for large-scale non-convex optimization. Our key idea is to combine momentum with adaptive step sizes via:

$$x_{t+1} = x_t - \eta_t \cdot m_t, \quad m_t = \beta m_{t-1} + (1-\beta)\nabla f(x_t)$$

We prove that this achieves an $O(1/\sqrt{T})$ convergence rate.
